Title: Leave Policy
 Employees are entitled to 18 paid leaves per year. Leave applications must be submitted via the HR Portal at least 2 days in advance.

Title: Reimbursement Process
 Employees can submit reimbursement claims by uploading receipts to the Finance Portal and filling out the reimbursement form. Approval typically takes 3–5 business days.

Title: Design Asset Request
 Design requests should be made via the internal design request form. Typical turnaround time is 2–3 business days.

Title: Remote Work Guidelines
 Employees must inform their managers at least 24 hours in advance when working remotely. Daily check-ins via Slack are encouraged.

Title: Travel Policy
 All official travel must be approved by a reporting manager. Bookings are to be made via the travel desk or the corporate travel portal.
